In all the battle royale games, looting is an essential thing to do because after you land on the map using the parachute, you have nothing but only your fist to fight. But your fist is not something you can kill many enemies as it will be only helpful in sneak attacks in close combat. 

So, you have to wander around the map from the point where you landed to loot items such as a backpack, a helmet, armor, medkits, and also weapons with ammo, and there are many other things you can loot. 

The more quality weapons and items you have, the easier the gameplay becomes. Therefore you should loot all the items you encounter in your gameplay. If you find a better item than you currently have, you should replace the current item with the better one, as almost everything you find on the battleground has a level. 

When you enter the battle with nothing to fight, your main goal is to find supplies or loot, but you should not forget to be careful when you wander around and loot, as many enemy players are there to hunt you down. 

Since everyone’s ambition is to be the winner of the match, you should be more careful when you loot because who knows when you will become a target of an enemy player? So we like to remind you not to act recklessly and carelessly. But always pay attention to your surroundings to ensure it is safe and sound so you can loot with fewer issues.
