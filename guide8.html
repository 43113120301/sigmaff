There is a set of loadout recommendations for you before you enter any mode in this Sigma game, and you can equip your character with these loadout recommendations to make your character much more prepared for the game. The loadout is two types, BR Loadout and Basic Loadout, and you have to purchase the loadout recommendations using the in-game currencies. 

The Basic Loadouts you can equip to your character are, 

  • Detector - to detect the approximate location of the opponents. 

  • Armor Crate - receives random armor with a random level.

  • Supply Crate - receives medkits and ammo randomly. 

  • Leg Pockets - increase the capacity to store items. 

 

The BR Loadouts you can equip your character are as follows, 

  • Summon Airdrop - summon an airdrop to the designated location. 

  • Bonfire - regenerates the HP of you and your teammates. 

  • Resupply Map - follow the instructions on the map to reveal a resupply point. 

  • Bounty Token - receives extra bounty when killing enemies while carrying this.
